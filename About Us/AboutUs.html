About Us

What We Do?

We are Techlearns provide technical trainings, workshops, seminars & guest lectures on all the latest trends of the Digital World. We conduct these workshops in online and offline formats depending on the availability of the students and trainers. We conduct these workshops and seminars free of cost in rural areas and schools.

Our certification courses are both industry and university approved, hence it gives more value to your resume and would help you in order to get an edge over regualr university students and interns.

Our Goal

Our Goal is to make education reach those who are in dire need of it. We aspire to uplift the educational standards of not only our city, not only our country but globally. We wish to erase the gap between industry and academics. We fill all the loop holes the university syllabus creates, hence making our students industry ready in no time.

We train you be become the best asset any industry of educational institution could wish for. Join us in our noble cause to make education reach each and every needy student.
